WELCOME TO LA CAMPAGNE

World of Conservation Education & Entertainment.


Welcome to la campagne tropicana beach resort, we are an african themed beach, lagoon and forest resort located in lagos nigeria. with over 65 acres of palm-fringed white sand beach, lagoon and mangroove forest providing ample opportunities for visitors to observe.

Experience warm african hospitality, comfort and luxury. our chalets offer a mix of traditional architecture and furnishing with all modern amenities you would expect in a 5-star resort. And if you prefer the great outdoors, we provide a range of camping options.
